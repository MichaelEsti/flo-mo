Studio Theatre

Studio Theatre is Washington’s premier venue for contemporary theatre, “where local audiences will find today’s edgiest playwrights”. Studio produces the work of today’s greatest writers, augmented by occasional productions of modern classics.

My team and I were tasked with creating a way for users to easily purchase tickets to their upcoming shows on their new website.

Plantagotchi

Plantagotchi is a conceptual application that allows people to care for their plants in a fun, and interactive way. Inspiration for this app came from the popular game “Tamagotchi”. Users create an avatar for themselves and their plants both virtually and in person.

My team and I created this application from the ground up. I was in charge of the UI, visual elements, page layout, and prototyping.
